Fun Facts About Socotra

Socotra is home to over 700 species of plants and animals that are found nowhere else on Earth.

The island is known for its unique dragon's blood trees, which have a distinctive umbrella shape.

Socotra has a long history of human settlement and has been ruled by various powers, including the Portuguese and British.

Socotra is a beautiful and unique island located in the Arabian Sea. Its breathtaking landscapes and diverse flora and fauna make it a must-see destination for nature lovers.
